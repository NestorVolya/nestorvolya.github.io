# Methods

## OSINT workflow

1. Define scope and hypotheses.
2. Separate raw sources from notes and conclusions.
3. Collect sources with provenance.
4. Extract entities, claims, dates, links, and contradictions.
5. Verify with multi-source checks.
6. Assign confidence level.
7. Produce a report, brief, casefile, or CV claim.

## Source confidence labels

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| verified | confirmed by public or strong local evidence |
| local evidence | supported by local files, exports, or logs |
| self-reported | stated in owned materials or profiles |
| needs verification | usable as lead, not as claim |
| classified / omitted | intentionally withheld |

## Principles

- Facts, mentions, assumptions, and judgments stay separate.
- Contradictions are investigated instead of flattened.
- Public claims need public links or clear confidence labels.
- Automation supports repeatability; human judgment remains responsible for conclusions.
